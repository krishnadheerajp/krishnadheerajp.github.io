Welcome to Graphics Processing Users Group

GPU:  

Our Aim & Objective

GPUG (Graphics Processing Users Group) Club aims to make every student familiar with the exploration of GPUs that play a gigantic role in various domains such as Parallel Computing, Machine learning, Deep Learning, Gaming, Augmented Reality, Virtual Reality, etc that the world runs on

What We Do?

We help students explore and gain interest in GPU technology and its significance in different domains by contacting leading companies in the field and conducting various events and workshops by collaborating with them. In this way, we support students’ journey in becoming experienced in GPU technology and in helping them to fetch jobs related to this field.

How It Started?

The GPUG club was established with the major goal in mind being spreading awareness about the GPU technology that has become a major part of various modern spheres of study. We decided to make this journey both fun and educational for students aiming to pursue a career in this area to understand and delve deeper into the technology that makes these devices as unique and useful as they are in many branches of technology such as machine learning, artificial intelligence, numerical analytics and many more. A group of students at VIT AP under the guidance of Dr. Nileshchandra Pikle formed GPUG which is now a full-fledged and well known technical club in the university.
