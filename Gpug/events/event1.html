Event Details

Hold on to your seat belts and get ready for a ride because we have planned the biggest event for the end of the academic year. Ciao Fiesta is a collaboration event conducted by three technical clubs: CSI, ACM and GPUG consisting of technical, non-technical games and quizzes open for all students at VIT AP. The games include a coding quiz, guess the word challenge, puzzles, and an exciting web hunt. There will be many interactive sessions where the students can come forward and share memorable incidents that they cherish. We have arranged a special session with seniors and juniors on job placement guidance for students looking for work after finishing their degrees. The best thing is that there will be prizes and free giveaways for active individuals who participate in the conversations and contest winners. The main aim of Ciao Fiesta is to bring together the students of VIT AP who have been separated due to COVID constraints and to have a great time to celebrate the end of the academic year with a blast. So, make sure you stick till the end.
